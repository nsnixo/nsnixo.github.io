Chapter 1: The Beginning

To the world, she was a successful art curator with an impeccable taste for the beautiful and mysterious. Chapter 2: Whispers in the Dark

The more Rida tried to ignore the photograph, the more it haunted her. She began seeing patterns where there were none, hearing whispers in empty rooms. Friends became suspects, and casual conversations felt like interrogations.

One rainy Tuesday evening, as she was closing up the gallery, a stranger approached her. He knew things about her past that no one should have known. His words were like poison, seeping into her mind and awakening doubts she had long suppressed.

"We all wear masks, Rida," he said, his voice barely above a whisper. "But some of us have forgotten how to remove them."

The night of the gallery's grand exhibition arrived‚Äîan event that should have been Rida's triumph. Instead, it became the stage for the final act of her unraveling.
